Animal, Sample, & Accucor Data Submission Process

Create a Sample Sheet

An important part of TraceBase is collecting and maintaining a consistent set of metadata for each sample.

  1. Make a copy of the Tracebase Animal and Sample Table Template.
  2. Fill in the Animals sheet more info
    1. Give each mouse in your study a unique id.
    2. Fill in additional details about each of the mice in your study. Note that there are comments in the column headers to help describe each of the fields.
    3. Pay particular attention to the Tracer Compound, Tracer Labeled Element, Tracer Label Atom Count, Feeding Status, Treatment, and Study fields.
  3. Fill in the Samples sheet more info
    1. IMPORTANT - Ensure that the Sample Name column is consistent with the sample names in your AccuCor files.
    2. Fill in additional sample details, making sure that the Tissue values match one of those in the Tissue sheet and the Animal ID values match one of those in the Animals sheet.
  4. Fill in Treatments sheet (optional) more info
    1. Provide a list of treatments used in the study, along with a description of each.
Optional: Validate your data

In order to expedite the loading process, you can check your data submission to resolve simple discrepancies by running your files through our automated validation script.

  1. Download your Animal and Sample Table in Excel format.
  2. Submit the table and any AccuCor files on the TraceBase validation page
  3. Fix any errors reported, re-download the table and validate the new Excel file.
    Note that in general, you should NOT edit your AccuCor files. It's simplest and often best to resolve issues by editing your animal and sample table file.
Submit your data on Google Drive
  1. Navigate to the TraceBase Study Submission folder on Google Drive.
  2. Create a folder with your name (if it doesn't already exist).
  3. Create a new folder for this study.
  4. Add the following files to the study folder:
    • TraceBase Animal and Sample spread sheet
    • Any AccuCor files that should be included in the study
  5. Submit the TraceBase Study Submission form more info
    The form will ask for the following information:
    1. Study Name
    2. Link to Google Drive folder with study submission.
    3. Mass Spectrometry Details
      1. Mass Spec Run Date
      2. Mass Spec Operator
      3. LC method(s)
      4. MS mode(s)
What happens next
  1. We'll review your study to ensure that everything will load smoothly. If there are any issues, we'll contact you to sort everything out.
  2. The study data will be loaded into TraceBase and we'll send you an email with a link to view the data to make sure everything looks correct and you can start browsing your data.

Contact us anytime if you have any questions, concerns, or comments If, after your submission, you need to follow-up or would like to check-in on the upload status, let us know.
