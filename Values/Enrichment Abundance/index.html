Enrichment Abundance

source

Found in the PeakGroups data format.

The abundance (ion counts) of a labeled atom in a compound detected in a particular sample.

enrichment_abundance = total_abundance * enrichment_fraction

See:

  • Total Abundance
  • Enrichment Fraction
